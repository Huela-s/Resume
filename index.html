About Me

Who I Am

I am Cheppalli Huela Sravya — a focused and determined student from Andhra Pradesh, balancing a dual degree with passion and purpose. I'm currently pursuing a BBA DBE at IIM Bangalore alongside a BCom at Osmania University.

Prior to IIMB, I was enrolled in the IPM programme at IIM Rohtak, which I chose to discontinue to pursue a path better aligned with my long-term goals and values. I completed my schooling at Bharatiya Vidya Bhavan's Atmakuri Rama Rao School.

A former national-level badminton player ranked 13th across India, I carry the same discipline and drive into everything I do. In my free time, I enjoy singing, drawing, reading, and exploring creative pursuits.
